About Our Shop

Bean & Treats is one of the oldest coffee shops in California. We hustle to make the best cups of coffee for our customers and we take pride on our ability to deliver great coffee and sweets at an affordable price.

We welcome you to stop by, along with your family and friends to enjoy a rich cafe ambience and lots of fun conversations.
